Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to understand how the platform coordinates between roles.

How do interns receive tasks?

Mentors create tasks directly within the Mentor Portal. These tasks instantly populate on the student's personal Kanban task list.

Who approves weekly progress logs?

Once an intern submits their weekly report, the assigned Mentor is notified in their Review queue to approve, request revisions, or add comments.

What parameters can the Admin monitor?

Admins oversee database accounts, manage student-mentor pairings, configure cohorts, and check platform-wide progress statistics.

How does our Android App (WebView) work?

Our Android APK uses modern WebView technology. Large apps (like Amazon or Swiggy) use this internally. It acts as a "Native App" shell wrapping a specialized browser that displays our portal with a native app feel.

🔥 Benefits (Pros)
  • Auto-Update: Any web updates instantly reflect in the app. No manual APK updates required!
  • Lightweight: The app size is extremely small (approx. 2-3 MB) for instant downloads.
  • Seamless Access: Direct home-screen access without opening a browser.
⚠️ Limitations (Cons)
  • Internet Required: A continuous connection is needed; offline mode is limited.
  • Micro-Delays: Slightly slower than a pure native app (Java/Kotlin), though practically unnoticeable.
